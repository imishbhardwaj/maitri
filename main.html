Sexual Violence Laws under IPC

Domestic Violence

Section

1. Domestic Violence – Section 498A of the IPC and Domestic Violence Act, 2005. 2. Assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty - Section 354 of the IPC

Punishments

1. Husband or relative of the husband of a woman subjecting her to cruelty — Whoever, being the husband or the relative of the husband of a woman, subjects such woman to cruelty shall be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years and shall also be liable to fine.

Online Harrassment

Section

1. Online Stalking - Section 354D of the IPC 2. Defamation (remarks on social media, obscene images or videos posted for public consumption) - Section 499 of the IPC. 3. Intimidating a woman by anonymous communication - Section 507 of the IPC. 4. Posting lewd comments against the will of a woman/requesting sexual favours (Sexual Harassment) - Section 354A of the IPC. 5. Transmitting / publishing obscene material in an electronic form - Information Technology Act, 2000 (IT Act) (Section 67). 6. Publishing an image of a person in print/electronic form resulting in a violation of the privacy of the individual - Information Technology Act, 2000 (IT Act) (Section 66E)

Punishments

1. Online Stalking - During the first conviction, imprisonment may extend to 3 years and fine, during second/subsequent conviction, imprisonment upto 5 years and fine. 2. Defamation (remarks on social media, obscene images or videos posted for public consumption) - Imprisonment upto 2 years. 3. Intimidating a woman by anonymous communication - Imprisonment upto 2 years. 4. Posting lewd comments against the will of a woman/requesting sexual favours (Sexual Harassment) - Imprisonment upto 3 years and fine. 5. Publishing an image of a person in print/electronic form resulting in a violation of the privacy of the individual - Imprisonment upto 3 years and fine

Rape / Sexual Assault

Section

1. Rape/ Sexual assault - 375,376,376A, 376B, 376Cand 376D of the IPC.

Punishments

1. A person accused of aggravated rape can be imprisoned from ten years to life along with a fine 2. In case of gang rape or one where during the act of rape, the accused kills the woman or puts in her in a persistent vegetative state, he can be charged with 20 years rigorous imprisonment to life, or be given a death sentence. The Supreme Court has defined persistent vegetative state as a state where the victim is alive but not aware of her environment.

Stalking

Section

1. Section 354D of the IPC

Punishments

1. First time offenders are punished with either simple or rigorous imprisonment of upto 3 years and a fine while repeated offenders are punished with simple or rigorous imprisonment of upto 5 years and a fine.

Ogling / Facial Expressions / Staring

Section

1. Section 354A of the IPC

Punishments

1. Either simple or rigorous imprisonment for a term which may extend to 1 year, or a fine, or both.
